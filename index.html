About Smiley Signer LLC

Experienced Notary Services

Reliable and professional notary and loan signing services offered to clients in Riverview, FL and surrounding areas. At Smiley Signer LLC we are knowledgeable and experienced in handling a variety of notarial acts such as acknowledgments, jurats, and oaths. 

Convenient Mobile Notary

We understand that our clients have busy schedules, which is why we offer mobile notary services. We can come to your home, office, or any other location that is convenient for you. Our goal is to make the notarization process as easy and stress-free as possible.

Affordable Notary Fees

We believe that notary services should be accessible and affordable for everyone. We offer competitive pricing for all of our services, and we are always upfront about our fees. You can trust us to provide transparent pricing and no hidden costs.
